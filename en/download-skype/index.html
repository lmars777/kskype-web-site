KSkype for Windows desktop

Thanks to KSkype and Skype, you can do so many useful and pleasant things! For example, you can tell your children a bedtime story, hold a meeting or take a foreign language lesson.

Here's a list of just a few useful features:

  • Free Skype-to-Skype calls.
  • Calls to mobile and landline phones at low rates and inexpensive SMS messages.
  • Video calling with Skype users.
  • Free instant messaging with friends, family and coworkers.

To get started, you'll need Windows 7, 8.0, 8.1, 10 or 11, a webcam for video calls and a microphone.

System Requirements

KSkype for Windows desktop

  • PC running Windows® 7, 8, 8.1, 10 or Windows 11 (both 32- and 64-bit operating systems are supported, Windows Vista and XP are supported with e.g. Extended Kernel fixes).
  • Internet connection (ideally a broadband connection, GPRS is not supported for voice communication).
  • Built-in or remote speakers and microphone.
  • For voice communication, it is recommended to use a broadband connection with a receive and send speed of 100 kbps.
  • For group video calls, everyone on the call will need KSkype 1.41.0 or higher for Windows or Macintosh and a webcam. Video quality is maximized using a fast broadband Internet connection (at least 4 Mbps receive and 512 kbps upload) and a computer with at least a Core 2 Duo 1.8 GHz processor. Minimum technical requirements: a fast broadband connection (512 kbps for reception and 128 kbps for upload) and a processor with a clock speed of at least 1 GHz.
