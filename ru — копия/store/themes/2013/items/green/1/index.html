Free Skype call recordings, animations

Now you can record your Skype video and audio calls of unlimited duration for free. Send winks (flash animations) to your friends, share videos, apply real-time video and voice effects. Easily keep track of your conversations.

  Features     Requirements  
  • Languages: English, Arabic, Chinese Simplified, Chinese Traditional, Danish, Dutch, Estonian, French, Hungarian, Norwegian, Portuguese, Russian, Spanish, Swedish, Thai, Turkish, German
  • Send flash video animation (winks) to your friends, family and co-workers. Choose from more than 35 available animations.
  • Free video and audio call recording of unlimited duration.
  • Choose from 3 video quality levels.
  • Create amazing video effects (Mosaic, Threshold color, Mirror, Emboss, Multiple images, Noise, Invert and Lens).
  • Transform your voice with Voice effects (Clone, Alien, Loud, Concert and Robotic).
  • Display chat text directly within your video conversation.
  • Display date, time, call duration and recording status in your video conversation.
  • Customize a different ringtone per contact.
  • Send emotion sounds to your contact.
  • Consult your chat log with the external Messenger Plus! for Skype LogViewer.
  • Preview and print any chat log conversations.
  • Search all your chat logs from one convenient interface.
  • Categorized your chat logs by dates, by sessions or by contacts.
  • Messenger Plus! toolbar can be conveniently docked to Skype and use a semi-transparent display in full screen mode.
  • Languages: English, Arabic, Chinese Simplified, Chinese Traditional, Danish, Dutch, Estonian, French, Hungarian, Norwegian, Portuguese, Russian, Spanish, Swedish, Thai, Turkish, German.

  • PC running Windows XP, Vista or 7, both 32-bit and 64-bit operating systems, Windows 8.
  • Internet connection - broadband is best.
  • For voice and video recording we recommend a computer with at least a 1GHz processor, 256 MB RAM and a webcam.
  • Skype 5.0 or higher recommended.
