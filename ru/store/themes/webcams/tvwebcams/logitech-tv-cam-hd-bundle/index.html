Features:

  • Plugs right into your big screen HDMI port and starts working right away on any brand TV with HDMI.
  • Logitech Fluid Crystal Technology delivers smooth video motion and richer, truer color.
  • Wide-angle lens capture everyone in the room.
  • Built-in Skype means there’s no software or computer needed. Just plug it in and call anyone with a Skype account — on their mobile, computer, TV or tablet.
  • The clip-on design makes it easy to securely mount on top of any HDTV.
  • A built-in ringer lets you know when someone’s calling — whether your TV is on or off.

Main features:

  • HDMI connection to any TV.

  • Carl Zeiss optics.

  • Skype built in.
  • Easy to use remote.

Note: Skype Premium is best suited for use on Skype’s Windows and Mac desktop clients where all of the features are available. The Logitech TV Cam HD currently only supports 1:1 Skype-to-Skype video and audio calls, voice calls to landlines or mobiles and group audio calls (as a participant only); Group Video Calling is not supported at this point in time. At this time, you can use your Skype Premium subscription with the Logitech TV Cam to cover your calls to landlines and/or mobiles in the single country of your choice.
